About Me

Hi, I'm Ankita

I'm a Melbourne-based makeup artist and beauty creator with a deep passion for making every person I work with feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

From soft, dewy bridal looks to bold editorial statements, I tailor every application to your unique features, skin type, and vision. Every face is a canvas.

I'm also a beauty content creator, collaborating with brands and photographers on UGC, TFP, and editorial projects.
